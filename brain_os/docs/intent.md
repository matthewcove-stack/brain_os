Brain OS exists to help a single operator organise
business and personal life by converting natural-language intent
into structured actions stored in Notion.

## Core goals
- Capture intent reliably
- Normalise intent into structured actions
- Execute actions deterministically
- Preserve an audit trail

## Non-goals
- General-purpose AI assistant
- Autonomous decision-making
- Multi-user SaaS
